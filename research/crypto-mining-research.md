### Mining Economics
- Difficulty adjusts every 2,016 blocks (~2 weeks)
- Recent 10% difficulty drop indicates miner exodus or reduced hashrate
- Profitability depends on: hardware efficiency, electricity cost, pool fees
- ROI calculation: (Block Reward × Block Frequency) - (Hardware Cost + Electricity)

### Profitability Factors
- **Hardware:** ASIC miners (Antminer S21, etc.) most efficient
- **Electricity:** Major cost factor (0.05-0.15 USD/kWh typical)
- **Pool Fees:** 0.5-2.5% typical
- **Difficulty:** Inversely affects profitability

## Ethereum (Post-Merge Status)

### Current Status
- **Consensus:** Proof-of-Stake (PoS) - No longer mineable via traditional mining
- **Staking:** Primary way to earn ETH rewards
- **Staking APY:** 3-4% typical
- **Validator Requirement:** 32 ETH minimum

### Alternative: Ethereum Classic (ETC)
- Still uses Proof-of-Work
- Mineable with GPUs
- Lower difficulty than Bitcoin
- Price: ~$25-30 USD per coin

## Solana (SOL)

### Current Metrics
- **Price:** $77-80 USD (volatile, ±80% realized volatility)

## Mining Hardware Comparison

### GPU Mining (Ethereum Classic, Dogecoin)
- **Hardware:** RTX 3090, RTX 4090, AMD RX 7900 XTX
- **Hashrate:** 50-100 MH/s (ETC), 500+ MH/s (DOGE)
- **Power:** 250-450W typical
- **Cost:** $1,500-4,000 per card
- **ROI:** 6-18 months (depending on electricity)

### ASIC Mining (Bitcoin, Litecoin)
- **Hardware:** Antminer S21, Antminer L7
- **Hashrate:** 200+ TH/s (BTC), 500+ MH/s (LTC)
- **Power:** 3,000-5,000W typical
- **Cost:** $5,000-15,000 per device
- **ROI:** 12-24 months

### CPU Mining (Monero, etc.)
- **Hardware:** Any CPU
- **Hashrate:** 1-10 KH/s typical
- **Power:** 50-200W
- **Profitability:** Very low

## Mining Pool Economics

### Pool Fee Structure
- **Standard Fee:** 0.5-2.5% of rewards
- **Payment Method:** PPS (Pay Per Share), PPLNS (Pay Per Last N Shares)
- **Minimum Payout:** 0.001-0.1 BTC typical

### Reward Distribution
- **PPS:** Guaranteed payout per share submitted
- **PPLNS:** Variable payout based on pool luck
- **Solo Mining:** 100% reward but high variance

## Profitability Calculation Framework

### Formula
```
Daily Profit = (Block Reward × Blocks Per Day × Coin Price) - Daily Electricity Cost - Pool Fees
```

### Example: Bitcoin Mining
- **Hardware:** Antminer S21 (200 TH/s, 3,500W)
- **Electricity:** $0.10/kWh
- **Pool Fee:** 1%
- **Difficulty:** 124.93T
- **BTC Price:** $63,800

**Calculation:**
- Blocks per day: 144 (at 10 min average)
- Daily BTC earned: ~0.0005 BTC (varies with difficulty)
- Daily revenue: 0.0005 × $63,800 = $31.90
- Daily electricity: 3.5 kW × 24h × $0.10 = $8.40
- Pool fee: $31.90 × 1% = $0.32
- **Daily Profit: $31.90 - $8.40 - $0.32 = $23.18**
- **Monthly Profit: ~$695**
- **Annual Profit: ~$8,460**
- **ROI (at $10,000 hardware): 1.18 years**
