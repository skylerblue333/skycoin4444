## DEMO TIPS FOR SKYLER BLUE

-   **Practice, Practice, Practice:** Rehearse the script multiple times to ensure smooth transitions and timing.
-   **Enthusiasm & Authenticity:** Your personal story and passion are your greatest assets. Let them shine through.
-   **Clear & Concise:** Avoid jargon where possible. Explain complex concepts simply.
-   **Show, Don't Just Tell:** Focus on demonstrating the features rather than just describing them.
-   **Eye Contact:** Maintain eye contact with the camera (or audience if live).
-   **Professional Setup:** Ensure good lighting, clear audio, and a clean background.
-   **Backup Plan:** Have a contingency for technical glitches (e.g., pre-recorded segments).
