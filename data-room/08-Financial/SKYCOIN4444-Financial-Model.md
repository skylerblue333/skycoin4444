---

### **1. Key Assumptions**

-   **Token Price Growth:** Gradual increase from Series A price ($0.05) to $0.10 (Year 1), $0.50 (Year 3), and $1.00 (Year 5).
-   **User Acquisition:** Phased growth, leveraging community building, marketing, and viral loops.

-   **Monetization Rate:** Blended average revenue per user (ARPU) across various revenue streams.
-   **Operating Costs:** Scalable infrastructure, lean team, and efficient marketing spend.
-   **Inflation:** 3% annual inflation rate applied to operating expenses.
