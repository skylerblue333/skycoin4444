## SKYCOIN4444 KPI Dashboard: Driving Growth and Impact

This KPI (Key Performance Indicator) Dashboard outlines the critical metrics SKYCOIN4444 will track to monitor platform health, user engagement, financial performance, and social impact. By focusing on these measurable indicators, we ensure data-driven decision-making, continuous optimization, and transparent reporting to our community, investors, and stakeholders.

---

### **1. User Growth & Engagement Metrics**

**Overview:** These metrics measure the expansion of our user base and how actively users interact with the SKYCOIN4444 ecosystem.

| Metric | Description | Target (Year 1) | Target (Year 3) | Target (Year 5) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Total Registered Users** | Cumulative number of accounts created. | 1,000,000 | 10,000,000 | 50,000,000 |
| **Monthly Active Users (MAU)** | Unique users interacting with the platform at least once a month. | 500,000 | 5,000,000 | 20,000,000 |
| **Daily Active Users (DAU)** | Unique users interacting with the platform at least once a day. | 150,000 | 1,500,000 | 6,000,000 |
| **DAU/MAU Ratio (Stickiness)** | Percentage of MAU who are also DAU, indicating engagement. | 30% | 30% | 30% |
| **Average Session Duration** | Average time users spend on the platform per session. | 10 minutes | 15 minutes | 20 minutes |
| **Feature Adoption Rate** | Percentage of users engaging with core features (e.g., SkyWallet, SkySocial, Hope AI). | 60% | 75% | 85% |
| **New User Retention (Day 7)** | Percentage of new users who return to the platform after 7 days. | 25% | 35% | 45% |
