## SKYCOIN4444 Beta Onboarding Guide: Welcome to the Future of Digital Life

Welcome to the SKYCOIN4444 Beta Program! We are thrilled to have you as an early adopter and thank you for helping us shape the future of this AI-powered Web3 operating system. This guide will walk you through the essential steps to get started, explore the platform, and provide valuable feedback.

---

### **1. Getting Started: Your First Steps**

**1.1. Account Creation & Login:**
-   Navigate to the SKYCOIN4444 Beta Portal: [Link to Beta Portal - *To be provided*]
-   Click on "Sign Up" and follow the prompts to create your account. You can use your existing email or connect via a supported Web3 wallet.
-   Verify your email address (check your spam folder if you don't see it).
-   Log in to your new SKYCOIN4444 account.

**1.2. Profile Setup (SkyProfile):**
-   Upon first login, you will be guided to set up your SkyProfile.
-   Complete your basic profile information, including a username, profile picture, and a brief bio.
-   Connect your preferred Web3 wallet (e.g., MetaMask, WalletConnect) to enable full Web3 functionality within SkyWallet and other modules.

**1.3. Explore the Dashboard:**
-   Your personalized dashboard provides an overview of your activity across the SKYCOIN4444 ecosystem.
-   Familiarize yourself with the navigation menu on the left (or top, depending on device) to access different modules like SkySocial, SkyWallet, SkyMarket, and SkySchool.

---

### **2. Exploring Key Modules**

**2.1. Hope AI: Your Intelligent Companion**
-   Locate the Hope AI chat interface (usually a chat bubble icon or a dedicated section).
-   Start by asking Hope AI simple questions about the platform or requesting assistance with tasks.
-   Experiment with its capabilities: "Hope AI, summarize my SkySocial feed," or "Hope AI, find a course on blockchain basics in SkySchool."

**2.2. SkySocial: Connect & Engage**
-   Navigate to the SkySocial module.
-   Explore your feed, join a few public groups, or create your own.
-   Post an introduction, share your thoughts, and interact with other beta users.
-   Test the messaging feature by sending a direct message to another user or a group.

**2.3. SkyWallet: Manage Your Digital Assets**
-   Access SkyWallet from the main navigation.
-   If you connected a Web3 wallet, you should see your assets reflected (testnet assets for beta).
-   Explore features like viewing your token balances, NFT collection, and transaction history.
-   *Note: For beta, please use testnet funds only. Do NOT send real funds to beta addresses.*

**2.4. SkyMarket: Discover & Trade**
-   Visit SkyMarket to see available digital and physical products (beta listings).
-   Browse categories, search for items, and simulate a purchase (using testnet funds).
-   If you are a creator, explore the option to list an item (beta functionality).

**2.5. SkySchool: Learn & Grow**
-   Enter SkySchool and browse the available courses.
-   Enroll in a free beta course and experience the AI-powered learning path.
-   Test quizzes, interact with the AI tutor, and provide feedback on the learning experience.

-   **In-App Feedback Tool:** Look for a "Feedback" or "Report Bug" button/link within the platform (usually in the footer or settings menu).
-   **Dedicated Discord Channel:** Join our Beta Tester Discord server: [Link to Discord - *To be provided*]. We have specific channels for bug reports, feature requests, and general discussions.

-   **Specifics:** Clearly describe the issue or suggestion.
